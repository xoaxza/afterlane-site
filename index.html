Build your uniform

Start with fewer pieces, worn more often.

Afterlane’s launch approach is simple: get the core formula right before expanding beyond it.

01

Choose a base

Pick the neutral tee or long sleeve that fits your weekday rotation.

02

Add one polished layer

Use an overshirt or shell to move from commute mode into the rest of the day.

03

Keep the palette tight

Dark neutrals and off-whites make repetition feel intentional instead of stale.

04

Train without changing the story

The goal is fewer hard switches between workwear and workout gear.
